Who we are?

We are Masjid Ibn Taymiyah & Islamic Center, established in 2003 to serve as a spiritual home for the Muslim community in the Greater Columbus area. Guided by the Qur’an and the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH), we strive to provide a welcoming environment for worship, learning, and community connection. Our masjid offers the five daily prayers, Jumu’ah services, and a peaceful space for reflection. We also host a variety of programs, including Qur’an and Islamic studies classes for all ages, a weekend school, marriage services, and ruqyah in accordance with Islamic principles. Through educational and social initiatives, we aim to nurture faith, strengthen families, and support one another in our shared journey toward spiritual growth and service to our community.
