OUR VISION

To Serve as a Home of Faith, Learning, and Shared Purpose for Muslims.

Masjid Ibn Taymiyyah & Islamic Center serves as a spiritual home for our community, offering daily prayers, educational programs, and social services that strengthen our faith and bonds.

We are dedicated to fostering an environment of learning, compassion, and unity, guided by the teachings of Islam and the example of our beloved Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him).
