Our Story

Established to serve the growing Muslim community in the Columbus area, Masjid Ibn Taymiyyah was founded on the principles of authentic knowledge and community dedication. Named after the renowned scholar Imam Ibn Taymiyyah, our center reflects a mission to be a beacon of guidance and hope.

From our humble beginnings to our current facilities at 2334 Mock Rd, we remain committed to fostering positive relationships with our neighbors and contributing to the broader community.
